# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 18, 2025**

Thank you for using our Countdown App (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

## 1. Information Collection

### Local Data
The App only collects and stores local data related to countdown functions, including:
- Countdown titles
- Target dates
- Countdown descriptions
- Selected icons
- Custom title images (if added)

This information is stored only on your local device and will not be uploaded or transmitted to any external server.

### Device Permissions
The App may require the following permissions:
- **Notification permission**: To send reminder notifications when countdowns expire
- **Storage permission**: To save and read custom title images

## 2. Information Usage

We only use the collected information for the following purposes:
- Creating and managing your countdown items
- Sending reminders when countdowns expire
- Displaying relevant countdown information
- Improving app functionality and user experience

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All user data is stored locally on your device
- We do not collect, transmit, or store any user data to cloud servers
- Your data is completely controlled and managed by you

### Data Security
- The app employs appropriate security measures to protect local data
- No network connection required, reducing data breach risks
- Users can delete or modify their data at any time

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The App will **NOT**:
- Share, sell, rent, or transfer your personal information to any third party
- Transmit your data to any external server
- Exchange data with any third-party services

## 5. Data Deletion

- You can delete any countdown item in the app at any time
- Uninstalling the app will result in complete deletion of all local data
- We will not retain any user data on our servers

## 6. Children's Privacy Protection

The App does not contain any content directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. App Features

The main features of this App include:
- Creating and managing countdowns
- Countdown reminder functionality
- Search and filter countdown items
- Multiple display styles (list, grid, card)
- Custom countdown appearance

## 8. Information Updates

If you wish to modify or delete your data, you can do so through the following methods:
- Edit or delete countdown items within the app
- Uninstall the App to delete all local data
