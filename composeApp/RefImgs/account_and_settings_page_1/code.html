Why create a cloud account?
expand_more
Creating a cloud account offers several benefits:
  • Sync across devices: Access your countdowns on your phone, tablet, and computer.
  • Secure backup: Never lose your important dates. Your countdowns are safely stored in the cloud.
  • Peace of mind: If you get a new device, just log in to restore all your countdowns instantly.
